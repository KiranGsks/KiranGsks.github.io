About Roko (short)

Roko is a Windows-centric, agentic AI application under development. It has two main components:

  • An open-source agent (C#) with Model Context Protocol and orchestration capabilities.
  • A unified integration platform focused on making tokenised AI accessible to everyone.
Our aim is a practical, privacy-minded System-wide Copilot experience. Roko is designed to be your competent copilot, working alongside you with borrowed brains from the best AI providers. It's AI that feels less like a tool and more like having a capable teammate who's always got your back.

About

This Retro Tennis project demonstrates our "foot in the door" approach; hands-on development that showcases privacy-first thinking, clean design language, and Neo-UX principles.

This game is our attempt at recreating the classic Pong game with a retro aesthetic and modern twists. We aim to evoke nostalgia while providing a fresh experience for players of all ages.

One of the First computer games ever made, reimagined for your smartwatch.

Experience fast-paced tennis action directly on your WearOS device. No phone needed*. Just tap, swing, and score. We want this to be a pure game as is, no data collection or unnecessary background activities.

FEATURES

  • Pure WearOS gameplay - play anywhere, anytime
  • Retro graphics with ultra smooth animations
  • Built to be light and fast even on older devices
  • Intuitive touch controls optimised for small screens
  • Offline mode available* - no internet required
  • Premium features unlock uninterrupted gameplay

PERFECT FOR

  • Quick gaming sessions on your watch
  • Fans of classic arcade sports games
  • Anyone who loves pick-up-and-play fun

PREMIUM BENEFITS

Unlock unlimited credits and advanced features through one-time in-app purchases. No ads, no subscriptions.

COMPANION APP

Pair with the mobile companion app to manage credits and sync progress across devices.

Retro Tennis is made for people of all ages. No personal data collection. See our privacy policy for details.
