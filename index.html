About Roko (short)

Roko is a Windows-centric, agentic AI application under development with two main components: an open-source agent (C# with Model Context Protocol and orchestration capabilities), and a unified integration platform focused on making tokenised AI accessible to everyone. Our aim is a practical, privacy-minded System-wide Copilot experience.

This Retro Tennis project demonstrates our "foot in the door" approach — hands-on development that showcases privacy-first thinking, clean design language, and Neo-UX principles.

Closed testing installation walkthrough
  1. Open the Play Store testing link provided above.
  2. Sign in with the Google account you want to use on the device.
  3. Join the tester group (there may be a Join / Opt-in button).
  4. On the target Android device, open Play Store and go to the app's listing. You should see an option to install the test build.
  5. If you don't see it immediately, try opening the link on the device or clearing the Play Store cache and retrying.
