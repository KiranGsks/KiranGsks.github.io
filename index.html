RetroTennis

Gyro-Controlled Sports • Wear OS • Public Launch March 2026

One of the first computer games ever made, reimagined for your smartwatch. Tilt your wrist to control the paddle — no screen touches required. Built from scratch for circular smartwatch screens with haptic feedback on every hit.

Open Beta: Join our testing community and help shape the future of smartwatch gaming. Pure gameplay — no data collection, no unnecessary background activity.
  • Pure WearOS gameplay — play anywhere, anytime
  • Retro graphics with ultra-smooth animations
  • Built to be light and fast even on older devices
  • Offline mode — no internet required
  • Premium unlock removes ads (one-time purchase, no subscriptions)
  • Companion app to manage credits and sync progress

Closed Testing Installation Guide
  1. Sign in with the Google account you want to use on the device.
  2. Join the tester group: groups.google.com/g/retrotennis-beta
  3. On your Android device, open the Play Store and go to the app listing. You should see the option to install the beta build.
  4. If you don't see it immediately, try opening the link directly on the device or clearing the Play Store cache.

Roko Sensor Engine

Our proprietary reusable middleware sits between raw sensor hardware and game logic, handling everything a smartwatch game needs to feel native and responsive.

Gesture Recognition Tilt, shake, rotation patterns with sub-frame precision
Haptic Management Standardized vibration patterns — haptics as UI, not just feedback
Battery Optimization Adaptive sensor polling — <2% drain per 10 minutes of play
Cross-Device Calibration Adapts to different wrist sizes, wearing positions, and watch models
70%+ Code Reusability
30% Better Battery
10× Faster Iteration
<5MB App Size

The engine enables us to ship 3–4 games for the cost of a single traditional watch game. We're not porting mobile games to watches — we're defining the interaction model for a new platform.
